Cast Iron Steak

Description

Nicely seared steak with hints of whatever herbs you would like to utilise to base with.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Salt and Pepper Steak
  2. Allow steak temperature to reach room temperature
  3. Heat up your cast iron pan blazing hot
  4. Fry your steak to desired levels
  5. Flip your steak 75% in
  6. THrow in your thyme and garlic with butter
  7. Use a spoon to scoop and base steak with godlike butter sauce
  8. Remove from pan and let rest for at least 5 minutes
  9. Enjoy its greatness
