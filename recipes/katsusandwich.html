Katsu Sandwich

Description

Succulent Pork Loin crumbed in crispy Japanese Panko wedged between fluffy soft white bread with a layer of crispy fresh cabbage to add additional texture.

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Pork Loin
  • Egg
  • White Flour
  • Japanese Panko
  • Cabbage
  • Kelpie Mayo
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Salt and Pepper Pork Loin
  2. Cover with white flour
  3. Cover with Egg
  4. Cover with Panko
  5. Deep Fry at 175c
  6. Thinly Slice Cabbage
  7. Layer sandwich with Cabbage followed by crispy pork
  8. Press Sandwich down with weights to compress
  9. Cut crust off sandwich
  10. Enjoy!
